Why switch to solar?

Over the past 10 years, the price of electricity in South Africa has increased by more than 300%. Yet there is no end in sight for consumers. More increases have been approved for 2019 as well as 2020. Load Shedding has cost billions of losses for the business sector and the South African homeowners are well aware of the inconvenience it has caused over the last 10 years. Decreases in pricing on Solar panels and the advancement in technology on batteries means that renewable energy options such as Off Grid and Grid tied has now become an affordable option for consumers and businesses. The maintenance cost on Solar renewable energy is very low as long as the components used are of high quality. Finance options are now available to make it more affordable for consumers.
